The Process

Seamless
From Start
To Finish.

We've engineered our repair process to be entirely frictionless. From the moment you drop your keys to the final handover, experience total transparency.

Start Your Quote

01. Request a Quote

Fill out our rapid online form or drive into our St Marys workshop. We provide comprehensive, no-obligation estimates based on cutting-edge assessment software.

02. Vehicle Assessment

Our master technicians conduct a structural and cosmetic teardown (if necessary) to uncover hidden damage, ensuring 100% repair accuracy.

03. Insurance Coordination

If claiming through insurance, we handle the bureaucracy. We liaise with assessors, submit the required digital imaging, and secure authorization on your behalf.

04. Repair & Respray

Precision panel beating, chassis realignment, and computer-matched painting in our controlled downdraft spray booths. Factory-level results, guaranteed.

05. Quality Inspection

A rigorous multi-point final check, full vehicle detailing, and polish. Your vehicle is returned to you looking flawless, safe, and ready for the road.
